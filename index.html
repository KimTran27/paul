about us

North American companies have been overpaying for consumable products for far too long. Import 2.0 was created with one purpose: to save these businesses money. We do this by giving direct access to the same suppliers that companies have been indirectly buying from for years. We do not skimp on quality. Instead, we simplify the procurement process.

Our stock is direct from the manufacturer, focusing on products that have been significantly overpriced for the past several decades. We are not shy about guaranteeing our cost savings and superior quality and aim to utilize technology to ease the overseas purchasing process.
